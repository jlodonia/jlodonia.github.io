How It All Began

Our Story

We first met during the chess tryouts for our college intramurals. What started as two strangers across a board quickly turned into something that felt like destiny. From the moment we played our first match, Jommar felt a spark — it was, for him, love at first sight.

What followed was a beautiful, winding journey: long conversations that stretched into the night, laughter that filled quiet corners of campus, friendly challenges over the chessboard, and the occasional tear when life tested us. Those years shaped us, helped us grow, and carried us all the way through college graduation together.

After graduation we began exploring the world, taking every chance to travel and make new memories. Each trip brought us closer, filled with shared discoveries, adventurous detours, and the comfort of knowing we could count on each other wherever we went.

Then, as a new year began, Jommar knew it was time. During a planned tour from Cagayan de Oro to Bukidnon, he asked Jhulandy to marry him with her parents and siblings there to share the moment. With a simple ring, a shaking voice, and a single, heartfelt “yes,” we turned that trip into the start of our next chapter together.
